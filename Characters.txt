BENJAMIN PRICE
Age: Late teens to early 20s.
Physical Description: Very tall, heavy-set, with dark curly hair and piercing blue eyes. He arrives looking visibly sick, pale, and sweating from physical withdrawal.
Wardrobe: Standard-issue gray hospital sweatsuit. (Arrives in an unzipped jacket with syringes).
Basic Mood: Quietly observant, defensive, deeply introspective, and weighed down by heavy grief.
General Attitudes & Arc: He uses biting sarcasm to mask his fragility.
Benjamin was a heroin addict who repeatedly stole from and betrayed his sister and parents; his guilt over this betrayal led him to jump off a roof. Because he is dead, he experiences no actual physical withdrawal in the hospital.
He serves as the story's emotional anchor and the primary skeptic trying to unravel the truth. His ultimate goal is to forgive himself for his betrayal so he can cross over.

REGAN
Age: Mid-20s to early 30s.
Physical Description: African American or mixed race. Radiates a grounded strength and warmth.
Wardrobe: Worn tee-shirt and scrub pants, often smudged with charcoal from drawing, with mismatched socks.
Basic Mood: Warm, sarcastic, and disarmingly self-aware.
General Attitudes & Arc: She masks her own depression and self-doubt with humor, acting as the caretaker and "big sister" to Benjamin.
She ended up in the hospital after overdosing on pills, which she claims was an accident. Without realizing it, she is the primary medium for the Ouija board, and the entity reacts most violently to her presence.
She will eventually cross over when she forgives herself for her overdose.

OTTO
Age: Mid-60s.
Physical Description: White, weathered, carrying himself like a soldier. Think James Marsters.
Wardrobe: Tattered WWI-style military coat over hospital pants, weathered combat boots, and fingerless gloves.
Basic Mood: Delusional, paranoid, yet disarmingly sincere.
General Attitudes & Arc: He believes he is a Private First Class stationed in WWI.
While he is treated as comic relief who speaks in outdated military jargon, his paranoia is actually highly tuned into the supernatural reality of the hospital. He knows the hospital is "containment".
He will cross over when he hears a radio announce a "ceasefire," allowing his war to finally end.

AMBER
Age: Late 20s.
Physical Description: Open ethnicity. Eccentric, enigmatic, and fragile.
Wardrobe: Faded tee-shirt with a cartoon cat, layered with scarves, mismatched jewelry, and bare feet. She always clutches a twine-wrapped stuffed cat.
Basic Mood: Anxious but spiritually attuned and observant.
General Attitudes & Arc: Amber is the group's spiritual guide. She knows the rules of the board and maps out the strange layout of the hospital.
She speaks in metaphors but understands the mechanics of the trap better than anyone. She is anchored by guilt over a lost pet.
She will cross over when she realizes "he found her" and releases her guilt.

CARMILLA
Age: Mid to late-50s (though she feels timeless).
Physical Description: A picture of messy, faded elegance. The "Broken Seer." Think Juliet Landau.
Wardrobe: Vintage velvet robe, a threadbare slip, long rosary beads, and chipped red nail polish. Always barefoot, wearing a dangling charm.
Basic Mood: Manic, lyrical, and tragically prophetic.
General Attitudes & Arc: She is the only truly insane patient in the ward. She constantly pieces together scraps of paper, mutters prayers, and whispers to mirrors.
She understands that the hospital spells things out, frequently warning that "the devil's in the details". She will cross over calmly after completing a final prayer.

JACE
Age: Early 30s.
Physical Description: Bold, intense, scowling. Looks like he is ready to run.
Wardrobe: Faded ‘90s band tee, distressed jeans, and scuffed boots.
Basic Mood: Angry, guarded, and defiant.
General Attitudes & Arc: Jace treats the hospital like a prison. He avoids group therapy, lurks in the halls, and carves tally marks into the walls to track the looping days.
Under his rage, he is fiercely protective and traumatized by the patients he has watched disappear. He refuses to cross over out of sheer anger.
The final scene reveals the Ouija board spelling his name, trapping him indefinitely.

HAROLD
Age: 60s.
Physical Description: African American. Frazzled, brilliant, and kind.
Wardrobe: Casual "suburban dad" vibe (casual shirt, slacks, worn sneakers), slightly stained with ink and graphite, thick glasses, rumpled suspenders.
Basic Mood: Warm, poetic, and obsessive.
General Attitudes & Arc: He is the hospital's scribe and clockmaker. He constantly draws chaotic diagrams to document the shifting architecture.
He understands the rules of time loops and provides crucial lore to the group. He crosses over when he finally remembers his wife, June, and her lemon perfume.

MALACHAI
Age: 12-15.
Physical Description: Open ethnicity. Slight build, barefoot, pale skin, dark curls.
Wardrobe: Standard teen clothing (hoodie, jeans, sneakers), but worn unnervingly, like a costume he isn't used to.
Basic Mood: Chaotic, intensely still, and menacing.
General Attitudes & Arc: He acts like a trickster spirit, staring without blinking, mimicking the voices of departed patients, and randomly screaming.
He converses directly with the walls/entity. Deep down, he is just a terrified child who accidentally shot himself with his dad's gun and wants his mother.

DR. ANNA
Age: 40s.
Physical Description: Ambiguous race. Serene, glowing, and just slightly off.
Wardrobe: Professional attire undercut by subtle, childish details: pastel buttons, a charm bracelet, jelly bracelets, star earrings, and worn flats.
Basic Mood: Hypnotically calm, though she easily fractures when pushed.
General Attitudes & Arc: She acts as the gatekeeper, but is actually a tethered vessel/projection of the teenage girl using the Ouija board in the real world.
Her dialogue is evasive; she prefers "yes or no questions" and always greets with "Hello" and leaves with "Goodbye" (the rules of the board). As the hospital deteriorates, her "teenager" persona bleeds through into her adult doctor voice.

CALEB
Age: Mid-20s.
Physical Description: Biracial, red hair, freckles. Implies a calm, deliberate presence.
Wardrobe: Implied medical/nurse attire.
Basic Mood: Caring, compassionate, and deliberate.
General Attitudes & Arc: He acts as the Head Nurse, but is actually an empathetic Angel/Spirit Guide.
He facilitates when and how people cross over, helping them solve their unfinished business. He never interferes if a patient resists the process.

THE ORDERLIES ("THE DOGS")
Age: Timeless/Unknown.
Physical Description: Towering, faceless figures. Predatory but guiding presence.
Wardrobe: Immaculate white scrubs with no ID tags. They wear a subtle dog-shaped pin and gloves with paw-pad stitching.
Basic Mood: Silent, perfectly motionless, dread-inducing.
General Attitudes & Arc: They are psychopomps (based on the Church Grim myth) who arrive to guide souls to the next stage.
The patients fear them, calling their arrival "sicking the dogs" due to the distinct clicking sound their shoes make on the tile, which sounds like claws.

LUCY
Age: 18.
Physical Description: Asian-American. Silent, still, and ghost-like.
Wardrobe: A long, flowing, vintage white gown, completely untouched by the dirt of the hospital.
Basic Mood: Silent, still, ancient, and dread-inducing.
General Attitudes & Arc: She drifts like a ghost and is ignored by most.
She is a Spirit Messenger who only appears to deliver cryptic, eerie warnings just before a major shift or veil breach occurs.

TEENAGE GIRL 1 (THE SUMMONER)
Age: 18-20.
Physical Description: Open ethnicity. Normal teenager in the "real world".
Wardrobe: Casual teen clothing (hoodie, leggings, bare feet).
Basic Mood: Curious, grieving, maybe a little reckless.
General Attitudes & Arc: A grieving girl in the "real world" who is unknowingly controlling the hospital and Dr. Anna through her Ouija board.
She eventually throws the board into a fire to end it.
